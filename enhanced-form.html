About Babynest school

Babynest school is dedicated to providing a balanced education combining academics, arts and athletics.

Key Features

  1. Student-centred learning with small class sizes.
  2. Comprehensive arts and sports programmes.
  3. Strong community and parental engagement.

Programs

  • Primary & Junior Education
    • Foundation Stage (Years 3-5)
    • Key Stage 1 (Years 6–14)
  • Senior Secondary Education
    • STEM Stream
    • Humanities Stream
    • Arts Stream
  • Extracurriculars: Music, Drama, Football, Arts
